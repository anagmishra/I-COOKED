Expense Tracker for Sustainable Development Goals

An essential tool for early-stage startups to manage and track their expenses efficiently.

Purpose

This Expense Tracker helps startups allocate their funding across various categories, track expenses, and ensure efficient use of resources. It aligns with the sustainable development goals by promoting financial responsibility and transparency.

Why is this needed?

Effective budget management is crucial for the success of any startup. This tool provides a simple and intuitive way to track expenses, making it easier to stay within budget and allocate resources where they are needed most.

Use Case

Imagine you have secured funding for your startup. This tool will help you allocate your total budget to different categories like salaries, business development, and team building. You can then track your expenses in each category and see a detailed report of your spending.
